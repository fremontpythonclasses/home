Who We Are

Fremont Python & Data Science Academy
Empowering the next generation with industry-ready tech skills
Led by a professional data scientist with a Ph.D. in Physics and over 8 years of experience in corporate and research environments, our academy offers high-quality Python and Data Science classes tailored for students in grades 5–12 and early college learners. We specialize in making complex concepts simple and engaging—helping young minds build a strong foundation in coding, problem-solving, and analytical thinking. Our hands-on curriculum includes real-world applications in data visualization, machine learning, and AI, preparing students for academic excellence and future careers in STEM. Whether your child is just getting started or ready to level up, we provide a supportive, expert-led environment to explore the world of data through Python.
